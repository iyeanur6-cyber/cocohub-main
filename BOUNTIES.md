# 💰 Cocohub Bounty Board

> **Earn XLM (Stellar Lumens) for building real features.** Payments via [GrantFox](https://grantfox.xyz) smart escrow — released within 48 hours of your PR being merged. No waiting, no chasing.

New to Stellar? Get a free wallet at [Freighter.app](https://freighter.app) in 2 minutes.

---

## How to claim a bounty

1. Pick an issue below and comment **`I'd like to work on this`**
2. A maintainer assigns it to you (first come, first served)
3. Fork → branch → PR referencing the issue (`Closes #N`)
4. PR reviewed within 3 business days
5. Merge → payment released from escrow within 48 hours

One bounty per contributor at a time. Bounties expire after **14 days** of no activity.

## 💡 Propose a bounty

Have an idea for a feature or fix? [Open an issue](../../issues/new/choose) with the `bounty-proposal` label. If we merge it into the board, you get a bonus 5 XLM.

## 📌 Rules

- One active bounty per contributor at a time
- Bounties expire if no PR activity for **14 days** after assignment
- Partial work may still receive a partial reward at maintainer discretion
- All submissions must pass lint, typecheck, and tests
- Payment requires a valid Stellar wallet address
