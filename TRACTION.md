## 💬 Why Cocohub exists

There are over **900 million pets** worldwide. When a pet owner moves, switches vets, or faces an emergency, their animal's medical history is either lost, on paper, or locked in a clinic's proprietary system. There is no portable, owner-controlled pet health record.

Cocohub solves this with a blockchain-anchored, offline-first, privacy-preserving pet health passport — owned by the pet owner, verifiable by any vet, accessible anywhere.
